wallets (for teachers and students)
id, related_user, currency (USD), cash - created for every user

payments
id, source_wallet, target_wallet, sum, status (in_process, canceled, approved)
Also create logging for payments


Payment algorithm:

1) Student send money from his wallet to teacher`s wallet
2) Create payment with status "in_process"
3) Payment sends to a payment system (stripe and etc.)
4) Payment system process a payment and receive a payments status (approved or canceled)
5) If status approved we set approved status for payment and withdrow cash from student`s wallet and add cash to the teacher`s wallet.
   If status canceled we show the error, and log it
